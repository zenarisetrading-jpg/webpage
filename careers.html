Open Role

AI Full-Stack Developer

On-site / Full-time (Min of 2-3 YOE Required)

Job Summary

We are building an AI-powered Amazon advertising technology platform and are looking for an AI Full-Stack Developer who can take features from idea to production. You will work across the stack — integrating AI into Amazon Ads workflows, building scalable APIs, crafting intuitive web interfaces, deploying to the cloud, and ensuring the platform is secure, reliable, and production-ready.

This role requires strong hands-on execution, strong product thinking, and the ability to move fast without compromising quality, accuracy, or security in a performance-sensitive ad tech environment.

Responsibilities

  • Build and ship AI-powered features for an Amazon ad tech platform end-to-end
  • Integrate AI models and tools into workflows for keyword research, bid optimization, campaign analysis, and decision support
  • Work hands-on with AI platforms and tools such as OpenAI, Claude, Claude Code, Cursor, Antigravity, and similar AI developer tools
  • Design and develop scalable backend services and APIs for ad data ingestion and processing
  • Build clean, responsive frontend interfaces for advertisers and operators
  • Own cloud deployment, CI/CD pipelines, and production environments
  • Implement authentication, role-based access control, usage limits, and billing hooks
  • Ensure application security, data privacy, and compliance with best practices
  • Perform security checks, code reviews, and vulnerability assessments
  • Monitor, debug, and optimize system performance, reliability, and cost
  • Iterate quickly based on customer feedback and business requirements

Required Skills

AI & Ad Tech

  • Strong hands-on experience with AI tools and platforms such as OpenAI, Claude, Claude Code, Cursor, Antigravity, or similar
  • Experience integrating LLMs, embeddings, agents, and automation into production systems
  • Understanding of AI cost control, latency, and reliability in real-time or near-real-time systems
  • Familiarity with Amazon Ads concepts (campaigns, keywords, bids, search terms) is a strong plus

Backend

  • Proficiency in Python or Node.js
  • Experience building REST or GraphQL APIs
  • Strong knowledge of databases (PostgreSQL, Redis, NoSQL)
  • Experience handling large-scale data ingestion and processing is a plus

Frontend

  • Experience with React, Next.js, or similar frameworks
  • Ability to build scalable, reusable UI components
  • Experience integrating backend and AI APIs into frontend applications

Cloud, DevOps & Security

  • Experience deploying production systems on AWS, Azure, or GCP
  • Familiarity with Docker and CI/CD pipelines
  • Strong understanding of security best practices (OWASP, auth flows, secrets management, rate limiting)
  • Experience securing multi-tenant platforms is a plus

Nice to Have

  • Experience with Amazon Ads APIs or Selling Partner APIs
  • Experience with vector databases (Pinecone, Weaviate, FAISS)
  • Knowledge of multi-tenant SaaS or ad tech architectures
  • Experience building decision-support or analytics platforms
  • Prior startup or early-stage ad tech experience

Why Join Us

  • Build a real AI-driven Amazon ad tech platform used by operators and brands
  • High ownership and impact from day one
  • Work at the intersection of AI, data, and advertising
  • Fast-paced, execution-focused environment
